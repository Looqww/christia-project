"My Whole Declamation Speech"

Hello everyone, I am Christina Sofia Marquez.

Do you think you'll be happy if you were criticized or discriminated against by someone? Of course not. Many people would not want to experience it. Today I am going to discuss about discrimination. Discrimination is the act of unfairness or prejudicial treatment of people and groups based on characteristics, such as race, gender, age, or sexual orientation. People should know that discrimination is not a right and that we should not do it. To the victim, you are not weak; your resilience in the face of such pain is an act of bravery. But to those who watch in silence, what are you? You hold the power to ignite change, to step up and say, “This stops now.” For every life torn apart by discrimination, there is a chance for healing if we choose kindness over cruelty. So let us end this cycle, for when we stand together, no one has to stand alone. Encourage people to discriminate against others. Discrimination should be stopped.
